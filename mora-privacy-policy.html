Privacy Policy for Mora

Effective Date: 2024-12-01

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Mora does not collect any personal information related to your hydration or usage within the app. However, we do use third-party services to track errors and improve the app's performance.

2. Error Tracking with Sentry

Mora uses Sentry, a third-party service, to collect and track error reports. This helps us identify and fix issues in the app. When an error occurs, Sentry may collect technical information such as:

  • Device information (model, OS version, etc.)
  • Error details (stack traces, crash reports, etc.)
  • IP address (for troubleshooting purposes)

No personally identifiable information is collected by Sentry. The data is used solely for debugging and improving the app's stability.

3. How We Use the App

Since we do not collect personal data, your water tracking experience is completely private and stored locally on your device. The error data collected via Sentry is used to improve app functionality and user experience.

4. Third-Party Services

In addition to Sentry, we do not use any other third-party services that collect or share data from users. We do not use analytics tools, advertising networks, or other third-party integrations that track personal information.

5. Security

Since Mora does not store any personal or usage data, there are no direct security concerns regarding your personal information. However, the error data sent to Sentry is protected according to Sentry's privacy practices, which we recommend reviewing.

6. Your Rights and Choices

As Mora does not collect personal information or store usage data, you do not need to worry about controlling or deleting any data. Your experience with the app is private and secure, with only technical error data shared with Sentry for performance improvement purposes.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any changes, we will update this policy and notify you within the app. Please review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we respect your privacy and handle data.
